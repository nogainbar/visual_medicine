Visual Medicine

About the technique

A soul's compass.

You ask a question and you get answers beneath the layers of logical thinking

A simple but powerful tool.

Whether you need to understand a difficult relationship or seemingly insoluble money or career problem, Visual Medicine can show you the creative-logic behind your thoughts.

A creative healing process.

Visual Medicine is a radically effective creative healing process that can unlock creative solutions for difficult-to-navigate personal, relationship, business, ecological and even global problems.

Visual Medicine is a practice that opens you to your creative right brain receptivity, to allow you to arrive upon solutions to problems in completely dynamic and surprising ways. Instead of using a traditional brush, Visual Medicine paintings are made by scooping acrylic paint onto watercolour paper.

This allows the Visual Medicine images to arise rather than being painted in the traditional sense, meaning the paintings come through you. Through the Visual Medicine practice you surrender your control and allow what is truly within you to emerge through the revolutionary language of the creative right brain.

The practice of Visual Medicine can show you how to lift the veiled layers of your difficulties and fears in order to find the meaning encoded within your psyche’s inner directive. Whether you are a beginner, experienced or even injured painter, as you practice you will go on to create literally thousands of paintings.

Often we look outside ourselves for answers and solutions to difficult situations, but in most cases we already know what to do. Visual Medicine enables you to unlock these answers and solve these problems through its creative mindful enquiry.
